What is research?

A systematic investigation to establish facts or gain new knowledge

What is the main purpose of research?

To answer questions or solve problems

What are the two main types of research?

Qualitative and Quantitative

What type of research focuses on numbers and statistics?

Quantitative research

What type of research focuses on experiences and meanings?

Qualitative research

What is descriptive research?

Research that describes characteristics of a population or phenomenon

What is experimental research?

Research that tests cause-and-effect relationships

What is correlational research?

Research that examines relationships between variables

What is a research problem?

An issue or question that the study aims to address

What is a research question?

A specific question the research seeks to answer

What is a hypothesis?

A testable prediction or assumption

What is the independent variable?

The variable that is manipulated

What is the dependent variable?

The variable that is measured

What is data?

Information collected for analysis

What is primary data?

Data collected directly by the researcher

What is secondary data?

Data collected from existing sources

What is a survey?

A method of collecting data using questionnaires

What is an interview?

A data collection method using direct questioning

What is observation?

Collecting data by watching behaviors or events

What is a sample?

A subset of a population used in research

What is a population in research?

The entire group being studied

What is sampling?

The process of selecting participants

What is random sampling?

Each member has an equal chance of being selected

What is a research design?

The overall plan for conducting research

What is validity?

The accuracy of the research results

What is reliability?

The consistency of research results

What is data analysis?

The process of interpreting collected data

What is a conclusion?

A summary of findings based on results

Why is ethics important in research?

To protect participants and ensure honesty

What is informed consent?

Participants agree after being fully informed
